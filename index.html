about Me

Max Mitchell

PROFESSIONAL TEACHING

I am a Guitar and Bass teacher, with over 9 years teaching experience, offering private tuition catering to all skill levels. I am also experienced in teaching singing as an accompaniment to guitar playing. 

Throughout my years of teaching, I have taught at various music schools as well as primary schools, while also maintaining my own private music teaching business catering to all ages and skill levels.  
 
Working with children certified 

Professional Experience

As a part of Melbourne’s thriving music scene, I have had extensive experience playing gigs throughout Victoria and Australia, highlighted by performances with International artists such as Terence Blanchard, Mirko Guerrini and Jim Black (AlasNoAxis). 

I am also experienced in music production and can use these skills to help students record their own music

TEACHING METHODOLOGY

I enjoy teaching all styles of music and am experienced in everything from Rock, Pop and Contemporary music to Folk, Jazz, Funk, R’n’B and Metal.

I pride myself on having a flexible teaching style and being able to adjust accordingly for each students preference. I also focus on helping students play songs and pieces of music that they choose, to help nurture and grow their passion for music while achieving their goals.  

EDUCATION

I have a bachelor’s degree in Jazz and Popular Music Performance from Monash University. And a partially completed Masters in Teaching from Monash as well.

TEACHING FACILITIES

I am based in Melbourne. For the student’s convenience I am more than happy to come to you for lessons.

I also teach from my home studio space where I also write and record music (in Kensington). 

Online lessons over Zoom are also available
